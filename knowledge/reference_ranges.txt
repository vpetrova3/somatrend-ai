REFERENCE RANGES — GENERAL HEALTH LABS
(Approximate adult ranges compiled from public sources such as Mayo Clinic, Cleveland Clinic, LabCorp and MedlinePlus. Values may vary by lab and are NOT diagnostic.)

CBC:
- Hemoglobin (female): 12.0–15.5 g/dL
- Hemoglobin (male): 13.5–17.5 g/dL
- Hematocrit (female): 36–46 %
- Hematocrit (male): 41–53 %
- RBC: 4.0–5.2 million/µL
- MCV: 80–100 fL
- MCH: 27–34 pg
- MCHC: 32–36 g/dL
- RDW: 11–15 %
- WBC: 4.0–11.0 ×10^3/µL
- Platelets: 150–450 ×10^3/µL

IRON PANEL:
- Ferritin (female): 20–200 ng/mL
- Ferritin (male): 30–400 ng/mL
- Serum Iron: 50–170 µg/dL
- TIBC: 240–450 µg/dL
- Transferrin Saturation: 20–50 %

THYROID PANEL:
- TSH: 0.4–4.0 mIU/L
- Free T4: 0.8–1.8 ng/dL
- Free T3: 2.3–4.2 pg/mL

BASIC METABOLIC MARKERS:
- Fasting Glucose: 70–99 mg/dL
- Creatinine (female): 0.6–1.1 mg/dL
- Creatinine (male): 0.7–1.3 mg/dL
- Sodium: 135–145 mmol/L
- Potassium: 3.5–5.1 mmol/L

DIABETES MARKERS:
- HbA1c:
  - Normal: <5.7 %
  - Elevated: 5.7–6.4 %
  - High: ≥6.5 %

LIPID PANEL:
- LDL (optimal): <100 mg/dL
- HDL (female): >50 mg/dL
- HDL (male): >40 mg/dL
- Triglycerides: <150 mg/dL

VITAMINS:
- Vitamin B12: 300–900 pg/mL
- Vitamin D (25-OH): 30–100 ng/mL

INFLAMMATION:
- CRP (high sensitivity): <3 mg/L

NOTE: These ranges are approximate and for pattern/trend description only. Always defer to the user’s actual lab reference ranges and healthcare team.
